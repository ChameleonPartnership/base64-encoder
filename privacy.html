Privacy Policy

Base64 Encoder Pro privacy and browser-only processing details

Privacy-first Base64 tools

Base64 Encoder Pro is designed so encoding and decoding happens client-side in your browser. Text, files, JWTs, and image data you process with the tool are not sent to a Base64 Encoder Pro server for conversion.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

What this site stores and processes

Browser-only conversion

Encoding and decoding operations run locally with JavaScript in your web browser. The content you enter into the encoder, decoder, JWT decoder, bulk processor, or image data-URI tool is processed on your device.

No server upload for tool input

Base64 Encoder Pro does not require an account and does not upload your input or output to our server for storage, logging, encoding, or decoding.

localStorage

The tool uses your browser's localStorage to save recent conversion history under base64-history and your theme preference under base64-theme. This data stays in your browser unless you clear it or your browser removes it.
